

Project Highlights:

📊 Sales and Profit Reports: Detailed analysis by customer, product, and region.
🔮 Sales Forecasting: Predictive analytics for future sales trends.
📉 Waterfall & Lollipop Charts: Visual breakdowns of sales by category and subcategory.
🗺️ Interactive Map Visualizations: Geographical insights with filtering capabilities.
🍩 Donut Chart: Showcasing profit distribution and regional performance.
📈 Orders vs. Returns Analysis: Line visualization highlighting returns by product subcategory.
📦 Interactive Product Dashboard: A comprehensive overview of sales and profits across products, regions, and states.
🧩 Linked Interactive Dashboards & Stories: Bringing it all together in a seamless and engaging narrative.
